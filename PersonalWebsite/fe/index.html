Workflow Creation for Asset Barcode Scanning Application

The Finance department required that I design the full workflow for the asset barcode scanning program. This involved developing workflows for the procedures used by the finance team as well as the app itself. I started with workflow design, moved on to UI/UX design, and finally took care of backend and frontend programming. For the backend and frontend development, I utilized .NET ASP technology, following the Application Development Standardization guidelines set by Pertamina. To address their feature requests and application requirements, I also held meetings with the finance team.
